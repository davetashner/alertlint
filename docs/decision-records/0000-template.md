# 0000 — Title (short, imperative)

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** who was involved

## Context

What situation forces this decision? What constraints apply (technical, organizational, cost)? Written so a reader with no prior context understands why doing nothing wasn't an option.

## Options considered

1. **Option A** — summary; pros / cons
2. **Option B** — summary; pros / cons

## Decision

The option chosen and the reasoning that tipped the balance.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what follow-up work this creates. Include negative consequences honestly — they are the part future readers need most.
